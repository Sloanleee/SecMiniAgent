# alert_triage
Description: Triage IDS, firewall, and industrial protocol alerts.

Use `parse_alerts`, `correlate_alerts`, `detect_bruteforce`, `detect_lateral_movement`, and `detect_suspicious_ot_access`.

When reporting, group alerts by source, destination asset, destination port, protocol, and severity. Highlight repeated authentication attempts, cross-zone access, and traffic to critical OT assets.
