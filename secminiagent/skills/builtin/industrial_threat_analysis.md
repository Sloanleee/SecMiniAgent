# industrial_threat_analysis
Description: Analyze industrial internet and OT security data with assets, alerts, IOCs, and vulnerability context.

Use `parse_assets`, `parse_alerts`, `extract_iocs`, `correlate_alerts`, `analyze_asset_risk`, `detect_suspicious_ot_access`, and `generate_threat_report`.

Prioritize defensive triage:

- identify affected OT assets and zones
- correlate suspicious sources, industrial protocols, IOCs, and known vulnerabilities
- explain evidence in plain language
- recommend containment, verification, and recovery actions

Do not provide exploit automation or offensive procedures.
